Block Flow (“the App”) is developed and maintained by an individual developer (“we,” “us,” or “our”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Block Flow iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account and we do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or precise location.

Note: The App uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically.

Third-Party Data Collection

Block Flow uses Google AdMob, which may collect information such as:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • App usage data
  • Crash logs and diagnostic information
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)

This data is used for:

  • Analytics
  • Performance monitoring
  • Crash reporting
  • Advertising-related functionality

We do not control how Google collects or uses this data. You can learn more about Google’s data practices here: Google Privacy Policy.

2. How Information Is Used

Information collected through third-party services is used solely to:

  • Monitor app stability and performance

  • Improve the App’s functionality

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

3. Children’s Privacy

Block Flow is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

4. Data Retention

We do not directly store personal data. Any data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective privacy policies.

5. Your Privacy Choices

You may be able to limit or reset ad personalization through your device settings, including:

  • iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
